\chapter{INTRODUCTION}

\section{Presentation of Internship}

I completed my internship at the \textbf{Research and Data Analytics (ReDA) Lab}, an interdisciplinary research laboratory under the \textbf{Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics} at the \textbf{Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC)}. The internship lasted four months, from 23 February 2026 to 23 June 2026. During this period, I worked in a research-oriented environment where data science, machine learning, and software development were applied to academic and practical problems.

The internship project was titled \textit{Python Libraries for Clusterwise Predictive Models: KFCProcedure and GradientCOBRA}. The project focused on the design and development of Python libraries for clusterwise predictive modeling and ensemble aggregation. More specifically, it involved two main components: \textbf{\textit{KFCProcedure}}, a modular framework for clusterwise learning, and \textbf{\textit{GradientCOBRA}}, a framework related to COBRA-based aggregation methods. These libraries were developed to support experiments on complex and heterogeneous datasets, where a single global predictive model may not be sufficient to capture different underlying data patterns.

ReDA Lab provided an environment that supported both research and practical implementation. It allowed students and researchers to work on data-driven projects while developing technical skills through experimentation and software development. Being part of this environment helped me better understand how machine learning methods can be transformed from theoretical concepts into usable computational tools.

Throughout the internship, I gained practical experience in both machine learning research and software development. My tasks included reviewing research papers on clusterwise learning and aggregation methods, analyzing existing implementations, designing a modular system architecture, implementing algorithms, and evaluating models using simulated and real-world datasets. Overall, this experience helped me connect theoretical knowledge in data science with practical implementation in a research software project.

\subsection{Objective of Internship}

The main objective of this internship was to gain both theoretical and practical experience in \textbf{data science} and \textbf{machine learning} through the implementation and evaluation of aggregation-based supervised learning methods. More specifically, the internship aimed to bridge the gap between statistical learning concepts and software implementation through the development of modular Python libraries.

The internship focused on the study, implementation, and evaluation of advanced ensemble and aggregation techniques, including \textbf{CombinedClassifier}, \textbf{\textit{KFCProcedure}}, and \textbf{\textit{GradientCOBRA}}. It also involved analyzing the relationship between these methods and related aggregation approaches such as \textbf{\textit{MixCOBRA}}. This work helped organize different learning and aggregation strategies under a common, extensible, and object-oriented architecture. Through this process, I improved my Python programming skills and strengthened my ability to design reusable, maintainable, and well-structured code.

To achieve this general objective, the internship was structured around the following specific objectives:

\begin{itemize}
    \item To study the theoretical foundations of supervised learning, including regression and classification tasks, and to understand the role of aggregation methods in improving predictive performance.
    \item To analyze existing source code and identify methodological similarities among MixCOBRA and GradientCOBRA
    \item To develop reusable Python libraries under a unified application programming interface and empirically test their performance.
    \item To evaluate and validate the performance of the developed libraries through algorithmic experiments on simulated and real-world datasets.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Duration of Internship}

The internship was carried out over a period of four months, starting from 23 February 2026 and ending on 23 June 2026. During this period, I was involved in research and development activities at ReDA Lab.

This duration allowed me to gradually understand the project, beginning with literature review and problem analysis, followed by implementation, testing, and evaluation of the proposed system. It also provided enough time to gain hands-on experience and adapt to the research environment.

\subsection{General Information of the Organization}

\begin{figure}[h]

As shown in Figure~\ref{fig:reda-logo}, the \textbf{Research and Data Analytics (ReDA) Laboratory} is a research and development laboratory under the \textbf{Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics (AMS)} at the \textbf{Institute of Technology of Cambodia (ITC)}. It is located in Room F201, ITC, Russian Federation Boulevard (110), Phnom Penh 120404, Cambodia. The laboratory focuses on research, innovation, and practical applications in data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and analytics.

ReDA Lab provides an academic and research-oriented environment where students, researchers, and faculty members collaborate on applied projects. The laboratory supports the development of intelligent systems and data-driven solutions for education, business, industry, and public sector challenges in Cambodia. In this thesis, the internship work was conducted at ReDA Lab under the guidance of the laboratory team.

\subsection{Services and Functions of the Organization}

ReDA Lab serves several important functions in academic research and innovation:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Academic Research Center:} conducting research in data science, artificial intelligence, computer vision, machine learning, and analytics.
    \item \textbf{Research and Development Hub:} developing prototypes, intelligent systems, and practical AI solutions for applied problems.
    \item \textbf{Student Innovation Platform:} providing opportunities for students to participate in research projects, develop technical skills, and apply theoretical knowledge in practice.
    \item \textbf{Collaboration and Knowledge-Sharing Center:} encouraging cooperation among students, faculty members, and external partners through structured research activities and laboratory projects.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Vision and Mission}

\textbf{Vision:} To become a leading research laboratory in Cambodia in the fields of data science, artificial intelligence, and analytics, contributing to innovation, education, and sustainable national development.

\textbf{Mission:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item To conduct research and development in artificial intelligence, data science, and related fields.
    \item To promote innovation through practical and applied research projects.
    \item To provide students with opportunities to develop technical, analytical, and research skills.
    \item To collaborate with academic, industrial, and public institutions in solving real-world problems using data-driven approaches.
    \item To support the responsible and effective use of AI technologies in Cambodia.
\end{itemize}
